This quote covers the development of a mobile application for the rehabilitation management platform. The app is primarily designed for **patients** to access their exercise programs, complete assessments, and communicate with practitioners. It includes **quality-of-life features for practitioners** to monitor patients on the go.

## DEPENDENCIES

The mobile app development depends on:

2. **Exercise Content:** Exercise videos must be available for testing
3. **App Store Accounts:** Client must have Apple and Google developer accounts

## ASSUMPTIONS

This quote is based on the following assumptions:

1. Web app backend (Firebase) is available or being developed in parallel
2. Exercise videos are provided in mobile-compatible formats
3. Standard Flutter packages and libraries are sufficient
4. No complex custom animations beyond standard transitions
5. English language only
6. Standard app store submission (no expedited review)
7. Client will provide app store accounts
8. Client will test on their own devices during development
9. Up to 3 rounds of revisions per major feature
10. No integration with third-party health apps initially

## RISKS & CONTINGENCIES

**Potential Additional Costs:**
- **App Store Rejection:** May require additional work to meet guidelines
- **Device Compatibility:** Some older devices may require extra optimization
- **OS Updates:** New iOS/Android versions during development may require adjustments
- **Scope Changes:** Changes to agreed requirements may increase cost
- **Video Performance:** Large video files may require additional optimization

**Mitigation:**
- Follow app store guidelines from the start
- Test on multiple devices throughout development
- Stay updated on OS changes
- Clear requirements documentation
- Video compression and optimization built-in
